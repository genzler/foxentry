Guidelines:
1. The quiz bee is open to all students under the college of information and communications technology.
2. The participants should register via the official portal of CICT on or before the last day of registration.
3. The college student council and organizationâ€™s officers are prohibited from joining the competition.
4. During the quiz bee, participants are not allowed to use the internet to research for answers.
5. Asking answers on co participants is also not allowed and is considered cheating.
6. Use of smart phones, tablets, cell phones, or other gadgets are not allowed.
7. Anyone caught cheating in any form will immediately be disqualified in the competition.
8. The quiz bee will cover all specializations under the college.
9. Each question will be either multiple choice, identification or modified true of false.
10. There will be three rounds. Effortless. Mediocre. Resistant.
11. Each round will have 7 questions.
12. Participants will be given 25 seconds for each question of each round.
13. The highest gainer of points will be declared as the champion and the 2 and 3 will be declared as runner ups.
14. There will be a clincher round in case of a tie.
15. Other participants will be given a certificate of participation after the quiz bee.
